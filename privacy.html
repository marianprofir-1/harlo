Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Harlo ("we", "us", or "our") is a reflection companion app. We take your privacy seriously.

Data We Collect

Harlo collects minimal data. We store anonymously: app usage events (such as sessions started and features used), linked to a random device ID. We never store conversation content on our servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it. We do not store the content of your conversations. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Conversations

Your conversations with Harlo are processed in real-time to generate responses. Conversation content is not stored on our servers after your session ends. If you enable the optional data sharing feature, anonymized conversation snippets may be stored to improve Harlo.

Third-Party Services

Harlo uses the following third-party services: Anthropic (AI responses), RevenueCat (subscription management), PostHog (anonymous analytics). Each service has its own privacy policy.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your local Harlo data at any time from Settings → Delete my data. For additional data deletion requests, contact us at privacy@harlo.app.

Children

Harlo is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@harlo.app
